My Story

DirectEd Scholar

Hey there, Gift here. Currently a DirectEd Scholar aspiring to work at a western tech firm. Born in the village outskirts of Kisumu Town, Kenya, I had a humble beginning and background. Currently, I reside in Nairobi City. I have always wanted to learn programming hence was really excited to hear about DirectEd and how they can transform my life. I decided to join their boot camp because this was once in a lifetime opportunity and through them, I will be able to achieve my dream to be a software engineer.

Education

Want to learn more about my education? I first undertook my kindergarten at Vison Academy. I later joined Good Testimony Junior School for my primary education where I managed to get 429 marks in my KCPE then enrolling in Mang'u High School. I was sharp and managed to get an A of 83 points in my KCSE.

Volunteer

My family and I love helping the less fortunate in our community so whenever we go to our rural area we always ensure to buy goods for our less fortunate neighbors and enjoy Christmas with them. I save up money during the year to buy them clothing and food to enjoy at Christmas. Back in High school, I was also a member of The Presidential Award Society where we would each contribute money to renovate a children's home, buy food for them among other things.
